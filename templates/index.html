About

The intern class of 2023 will be raising money for three amzing causes. Each cause is supported by one of three teams made up of the Pride's 2023 interns. Our collective goal is to raise $10,000 for the MRI Software Foundation.

Team Charaties


Team Golden Hearts

Supporting the Meals of Hope Organization


An organization that has packed over 80 million shelf stable meals that are donated to communities both in the US an globally.

Campaign Crusaders

Supporting the Cleveland Chapter of Boys and Girls Club of America

Provides a safe place for children in pverty to learn and grow, surrponding them with caring mentors.

Back On Our Paws

Supporting the Back on My Feet Organization


Combats homelesness through the power of fitness, community support and essential employment and housing resources.
